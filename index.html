About Me

I am a Full-Stack Electronics Engineer with over 15 years of experience spanning Hardware Design, Firmware Development, and Software Engineering. My core expertise lies in building systems from the silicon level upward, with a strong focus on low-level architectures and performance-optimized embedded solutions.

I have led multiple projects from concept and schematic design through PCB layout, prototyping, production-ready firmware, DFM optimization, and regulatory compliance, including EMI certification. I take pride in delivering robust, scalable, and production-grade solutions.

More About Me

I am passionate about designing complete hardware systems — from component selection and PCB layout to board bring-up and firmware architecture. There is nothing more rewarding than powering a board for the first time and watching the very first line of firmware execute successfully. I enjoy taking products all the way to advanced stages such as secure over-the-air firmware updates and edge-based machine learning inference.

My journey began in analog electronics during my teenage years, building discrete circuits and exploring signal behavior. I later transitioned into digital electronics, developing a strong foundation in microcontroller architectures, instruction sets, and low-level programming. Working directly with Assembly language allowed me to deeply understand memory organization, interrupts, timers, watchdog systems, and embedded runtime behavior.

Over time, I expanded into higher-level software development, progressively mastering C, C++, Objective-C, Python, Swift, and Rust. This layered progression — from bare-metal systems to modern software ecosystems — enables me to bridge hardware and software seamlessly.

I have been developing native iOS applications since 2011, delivering robust and scalable applications across multiple generations of Apple’s ecosystem. I specialize in native iOS development using Objective-C and Swift, and I also build cross-platform applications using Flutter. Since adopting Flutter in 2019, it has become my preferred framework for developing high-performance, maintainable mobile solutions.

My expertise spans the entire technology stack — from passive components and discrete analog circuits to transistors, timers, operational amplifiers, logic gates, FPGA systems, instruction set architectures, compilers, operating systems, runtime environments, and modern application frameworks.
