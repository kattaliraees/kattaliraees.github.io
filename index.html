Bridging Two Worlds

I’m a cross-disciplinary systems engineer with over 15 years of experience delivering robust products that span from bare silicon up to production software layers.

My background bridges analog hardware design, bare-metal/RTOS-based firmware architectures, and native mobile frontends. This synthesis allows me to architect cohesive, tightly integrated products, eliminating the traditional friction interface between hardware design, low-level firmware engineering, and application software.

I specialize in bringing designs from initial sketch, schematic, and board layout, through component selection, prototyping, DFM optimization, and EMI certification, straight to mass production. On the software side, I write highly performant systems code in C, C++, and Rust, while shipping seamless end-user applications using Swift for iOS and Flutter/Dart for cross-platform products.
